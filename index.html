CV (PDF)»

Dr. Aditya Nigam received his Masters (M.Tech) and Doctoral (Ph.D) degrees from Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur in 2009 and 2014 respectively. Presentely he is working as an Associate Professor at IIT Mandi, HP in the School of Computing and Electrical Engineering (SCEE). He joined this School during August 2014 as a Teaching Fellow.

His research interests include Deep Learning, Biometrics, Medical Image Processing & its Analysis, Image Processing, Computer Vision & Machine Learning.
